# Wireless Network Optimization

**Source:** https://www.ikuai8.com/support/ymgn/lyym/ac/2022-07-11-07-47-32.html

## I. Overview

Wireless Network Optimization automatically adjusts the channel and signal strength settings of APs to improve overall wireless performance.

**Requirements:** Router version 3.6.4 or higher.

---

## II. Optimization Options

Administrators can choose what to optimize:

- **Channel optimization** — Automatically selects the best channel to reduce interference.
- **Signal strength optimization** — Adjusts transmit power for better coverage.
- **Frequency band scope** — Choose to optimize 2.4G only, 5G only, or both bands simultaneously.

---

## III. Optimization Steps

1. From the supported AP list, select the devices to optimize and add them to the optimization queue.
2. Click **Start Optimization** to begin the automated process.
3. Monitor the real-time optimization progress percentage.
4. When complete, review the proposed channel adjustment plan.
5. Click **Apply Configuration** to push the new channel parameters to the selected APs.
6. View past optimization runs via **Optimization Records**.

---

## IV. Important Note

Wireless connectivity may be **briefly interrupted** on affected APs during the optimization process.
