## I. Overview

The Cache Status page displays the operational state of the caching function after it has been enabled. It provides two key metrics:

---

## II. Metrics

### Cache Status

Shows the traffic currently cached on the cache disk and how much bandwidth was saved through caching.

### Hit Rate

The hit rate is the probability that data requested by the local network matches data already stored in the cache.

A higher hit rate means more requests are being served from the local cache rather than fetching content from the internet, resulting in greater bandwidth savings.

> See [Cache Settings](cache-settings.md) to configure and enable the caching function.
